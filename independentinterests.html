Some Artists that inspire me:
Stephen Shore
This Image is from his series called Uncommon Places. He travelled across america photographing forget regions.
Nan Goldin Goldin became famous for her breathtaking series on domestic abuse, the Ballad of Sexual Dependency. Throughout her career she focussed on capturing counterculture movements.
Laura Greenfeild Greenfeild has been creating photography and video projects her whole career attempting to understand American culture. She created a series called Generation Wealth which follows the growing gap between the classes.
